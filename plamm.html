Plam

An innovative solution for early detection of the Red Palm Weevil (RPW) using advanced acoustic sensors and smart algorithms. It monitors the health status of palm trees (suspected, infected, under treatment, or healthy) and can detect RPW at a very early stage with 95% accuracy from day one.

Not just monitoring. smart early detection with 95% accuracy

How It Works

01.

02.

Analyzes more than 15 key parameters using sophisticated algorithms.

03.

Detects RPW larvae in the early stages, even before they are visible or detectable by humans.
